Kućni red

Please respect the house rules to make your stay as pleasant as possible. When leaving the property, lock the front door, close the sun umbrellas, turn off the lights and all electrical appliances, and turn off the water. The keys must be returned to the host upon checkout.

CHECKOUT: by 10:00 AM

Maximum number of guests

  • 10 guests. Bringing other people who are not registered as guests is prohibited.
  • Organizing parties and gatherings is strictly prohibited.

Pets & smoking

  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Smoking inside the house is prohibited. Smoking is allowed outside, with butts disposed of in ashtrays.

Pool use

  • Pool use is at your own risk. Children must be supervised by adults at all times.

Equipment and furniture

  • Please handle the equipment and furniture with care, and report any damage to the host immediately.
  • It is forbidden to take devices and equipment (towels, blankets, etc.) out of the house.

Fire safety

  • It is not allowed to bring weapons, explosives, or flammable materials into the house.
  • The owner has the right to enter the house in the guest's absence in exceptional circumstances to prevent damage or danger, and will inform the guest at the first next contact.

Liability

  • The owners are not responsible for any injuries to guests or theft of personal belongings.

In case of non-compliance with house rules, the host has the right to cancel further services to the guest. Thank you!
