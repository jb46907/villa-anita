Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Villa Anita collects only the information necessary to respond to your enquiries and process dinner reservations. We do not sell, share, or misuse your personal data.

1. Who we are

Villa Anita is a private holiday villa located in Dalmatia, Croatia. This privacy policy explains how we handle personal data collected through our website (villaanita.github.io or any connected domain).

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: info.luxuryvillaanita@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily submit a form on our website. This includes:

  • Full name
  • Email address or phone number
  • Requested dates and number of guests
  • Meal preferences and special requests
  • Review content, name and country (if you submit a review)

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No data is collected passively as you browse.

3. How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your accommodation or dinner enquiry
  • Confirm and manage your reservation
  • Publish your review on our website (with your consent, after approval)

We will never use your data for marketing, newsletters, or pass it to third parties for commercial purposes.

4. How your data is processed

Form submissions are handled through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form processing service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted securely to Formspree and forwarded to our email inbox. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to data in transit — you can read it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not operate a database. Submitted data is stored only in our email inbox and is not shared beyond that.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your reservation enquiry
  • Legitimate interest — responding to your questions and managing bookings
  • Consent — publishing reviews you voluntarily submit

6. Your rights

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. request removal of a published review)

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at info.luxuryvillaanita@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

We retain enquiry and reservation data in our email inbox for up to 2 years for record-keeping purposes, after which it is deleted. Published reviews may remain on the website until you request their removal.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We recommend checking this page occasionally.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:
